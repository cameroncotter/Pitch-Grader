# Pitch Grader — Deploy to Vercel

Statcast-powered pitch grading system. Follow these steps to get it live on your phone.

---

## Step 1: Upload to GitHub

1. Go to **github.com** and sign in (or create a free account)
2. Click the **+** icon → **New repository**
3. Name it `pitch-grader`, keep it Public, click **Create repository**
4. On the next page click **uploading an existing file**
5. Drag and drop **all the files and folders** from this zip into the upload area
6. Click **Commit changes**

---

## Step 2: Deploy on Vercel

1. Go to **vercel.com** and click **Sign Up** → continue with GitHub
2. Click **Add New Project**
3. Find `pitch-grader` in the list and click **Import**
4. Leave all settings as default — Vercel auto-detects Vite
5. Click **Deploy**
6. Wait ~1 minute — you'll get a live URL like `pitch-grader.vercel.app`

---

## Step 3: Add to your phone home screen

**iPhone (Safari only):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow at bottom)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** — done!

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three dots** menu (top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** — done!

The app will appear on your home screen with the Pitch Grader icon and open full screen like a native app.
